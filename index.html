Our Materials

Treading lighter, together

Natural Materials

At MINIME, we believe in using materials that are better for your feet and the planet. Every product is made with renewable, sustainable materials that require less energy and water to produce.

🌿

Merino Wool

Soft, breathable, and naturally odor-resistant. Our ZQ-certified merino wool comes from ethical farms in New Zealand.

🌳

Eucalyptus Tree Fiber

Silky smooth and cooling. Made from sustainably harvested eucalyptus trees using a closed-loop production process.

🌾

Sugarcane

Our SweetFoam™ soles are made from Brazilian sugarcane, the world's first carbon-negative green EVA.

♻️

Recycled Materials

We use recycled polyester, recycled nylon, and other post-consumer waste to reduce our environmental impact.

Carbon Neutral

We measure our entire carbon footprint—from materials to manufacturing to shipping—and offset it to zero by investing in renewable energy and carbon capture projects.

  • 100% renewable energy in our facilities
  • Carbon-neutral shipping on every order
  • Investment in verified offset projects
  • Transparent reporting on our progress

Circular Design

We design our products to last longer and live multiple lives. When your shoes reach the end of their life, send them back to us and we'll recycle them into new products.

Our Story

How MINIME began

It Started With a Simple Question

Why are all shoes so uncomfortable after a few hours? Our founders, frustrated with blisters and sore feet, set out to create footwear that you could wear all day without thinking about your feet.

After years of research and testing, MINIME was born in 2015. We combined natural materials like merino wool and eucalyptus fibers with innovative design to create shoes that are both comfortable and sustainable.

Our Mission

We believe that comfort and sustainability shouldn't be compromises. Every product we create is designed to be better for your feet and better for the planet.

From our carbon-neutral shipping to our renewable materials, we're committed to making choices that reduce our environmental impact while delivering products you'll love wearing every day.

Looking Forward

We're constantly innovating and finding new ways to make our products even better. Whether it's discovering new sustainable materials or improving our manufacturing processes, we're always working towards a better future.

Thank you for being part of our journey. Every purchase supports our mission to create better products in a better way.

Returns & Exchanges

Easy, free returns within 30 days

Our Return Policy

We want you to love your MINIME products. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return or exchange your items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

How to Return

1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"

2. Select the items you'd like to return

3. Print your prepaid return shipping label

4. Pack your items and drop off at any carrier location

Return Requirements

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached. We understand you need to try them on, but please ensure they haven't been worn outside or show any signs of wear.

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we'll inspect the items and process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Exchanges

To exchange an item for a different size or color, simply return your original item and place a new order. This ensures you get your new item as quickly as possible.

Questions?

Contact our customer service team at returns@minime.com or call 1-800-MINIME-1.

Shipping Information

Fast, free, carbon-neutral delivery

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the United States. Orders under $75 have a $10 shipping fee.

Shipping Times

Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days

Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($15)

Next Day: 1 business day ($25)

International Shipping

We currently ship to Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, and select European countries. International shipping times vary by location, typically 7-14 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing "My Orders".

Carbon Neutral Shipping

All MINIME shipments are carbon neutral. We measure the carbon emissions from every delivery and offset 100% through verified carbon reduction projects.

Questions?

Accessibility

Our commitment to digital accessibility

Our Commitment

MINIME is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Measures to Support Accessibility

MINIME takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff
  • Assign clear accessibility goals and responsibilities
  • Employ formal accessibility quality assurance methods

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:

Email: accessibility@minime.com

Phone: 1-800-MINIME-1

We try to respond to feedback within 2 business days.

Technical Specifications

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard navigation

Assessment Approach

MINIME assessed the accessibility of this website by the following approaches:

  • Self-evaluation
  • External evaluation
  • Automated testing tools
  • Manual testing with assistive technologies
